**Admission Process for Diploma Courses at Government Polytechnic College, Mandsaur**

Admission process for diploma courses at Government Polytechnic College, Mandsaur is conducted through a centralized counseling process by the Directorate of Technical Education, Madhya Pradesh.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- 10th pass with 35% marks for admission to 1st semester
- 10th pass with 40% marks for admission to 2nd semester (Lateral Entry)

**Admission Process:**

1. **Online Registration:** Candidates need to register themselves on the online admission portal of DTE MP: https://dte.mponline.gov.in/portal/services/onlinecounselling/counshomepage/home.aspx
2. **Online Counseling:** Based on the merit list, candidates will be called for online counseling. Candidates need to choose their preferred college and course during counseling.
3. **Document Verification:** After online counseling, candidates need to report to the allotted college for document verification.
4. **Admission Confirmation:** After document verification, candidates will be confirmed for admission.

**Important Documents:**

- 10th Mark sheet
- 10th Certificate
- Caste Certificate (if applicable)
- Income Certificate (if applicable)
- ID Proof (Aadhaar Card, Pan Card, etc.)

**Helpdesk:**

For any queries or assistance, you can contact the Directorate of Technical Education, Madhya Pradesh or visit the official website: https://dte.mponline.gov.in/

**Note:**

- Admission process and eligibility criteria may change as per the directives of the Directorate of Technical Education, Madhya Pradesh.
- Candidates are advised to check the official website of DTE MP for the latest updates and notifications.
